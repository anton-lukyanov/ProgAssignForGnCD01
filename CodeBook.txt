
Original dataset "Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Data Set"
(http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones)

Tidy dataset created from original by aggregating measurements on activity type, subject number, measurement type(variable).

1) "Activity type" can be:
 - WALKING

 - WALKING_UPSTAIRS
 - WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
 - SITTING

 - STANDING
 - LAYING

2) "Subject" contains numbers from 1 to 30 - represents study participants.
3) "Variable" means type of measurement - 11 means and std of body behaviour by 3 coordinates. Total 66 variables.
